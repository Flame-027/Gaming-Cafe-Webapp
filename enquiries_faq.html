The Experience
Q: What is a gaming lounge?
A: A gaming lounge is a centre where patrons can play games on high-end PCs and both retro and modern consoles for an hourly fee. It provides a social meeting area for gamers to play together.
Q: When should we arrive?
A: It is advised that you arrive no later than 10 minutes prior to the scheduled reservation so that players can make full use of the time requested.
Health & Accessibility
Q: Is there disability access?
A: We have wheelchair access to the venue and staff are available to assist wherever possible for disabled patrons.
Q: What are the fire safety procedures?
A: In the case of a fire, staff will provide instructions and guidance to emergency exits so that the safety of players is ensured.
Snacks & Drinks
Q: Are there any allergy-free and vegan options offered?
A: We have many food options that are allergy-free and vegan-friendly so do not worry about that!
Q: Will there be any alcoholic drinks available?
A: There will be alcoholic drinks available at the bar. Patrons may be required to present ID prior to ordering.
