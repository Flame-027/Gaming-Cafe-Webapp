Big Birthday (Online) Bash!!

by Game Cafe | July 10 | Uncategorized | 0 comments |

**WIN A BOARD GAME!**

We can’t celebrate our birthday with you in person so we have lots of online birthday treats in store for you!

Get started by liking and sharing our Facebook post to be entered into the first of our Big Birthday Bash competitions! Comment to let us know you’ve done it as well as which game you would prefer and you’ll be entered into a draw to win one of the below board games! Bonus points for a funny lockdown GIF!

For these 4 consecutive days we’ll be announcing 4 competitions to win one of these games. Winners will be announced on a Facebook live stream on 4th Birthday on January 26th at 3pm. Check in for a new competition each day!

Later that day we’ll be starting our Big Birthday Bash sale which will run 26th – 31st Jan. Use code ‘4YEARSOLD’ TO GET 5% off every store item! (Members message us for an additional members discount code!). There will be other items for sale at a deeper discount as well as flash sales on new items each day! So keep your eyes on our social media for our Big Birthday deals!

If restriction dont't lift...

Should Jan 19th come and go with restrictions still in place then we will postpone our Free Gaming Week until the next date restrictions are promised to be lifted. However we won’t leave you totally high and dry! We shall still have free gaming all day on Tuesday 20th so we can all sulk together!
